Services

Kiran Associates is a custom clearance and forwarding company and also offers import export

Custom Clearance Services

Custom clearance in Pakistan refers to the procedures and documentation required by customs authorities to allow the entry or exit of goods, including declaration, assessment, payment, inspection, and issuance of clearance certificate.

FREIGHT FORWARDING Services

Freight forwarding involves the coordination and management of international shipping and logistics operations, including arranging transportation, documentation, customs clearance, and other related services for the movement of goods across borders on behalf of importers and exporters.

IMPORT & EXPORT Services

Import refers to bringing goods into the country, while export refers to sending goods out of the country, both involving customs procedures and compliance with regulations.

TRANSPORTATION Services

Port-to-warehouse transportation services involve the movement of goods from a port to a designated warehouse, provided by logistics companies for efficient transfer and storage of goods.
