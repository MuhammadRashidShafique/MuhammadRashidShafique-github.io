About Us

Kiran Associates is a well-operated Customs Bonded Carriers & Transporters Company operating since 2001. The company operates via nationwide facilities and an overseas network of agents that service every corner of Pakistan. We have developed a solid reputation within the Pakistan Trade Community as a capable can-do Customs Bonded Carrier, Customs Clearing & Forwarding, and Transporter, no matter what the task is. People are our biggest assets. Our professional squad is skilled and competent enough in their respective extent that any shipping and customs tribulation is handled and overcome by them smartly, shielding our clients with a risk-free factor. Leveraging on the extensive logistics and skills, our team can create cost- effective solutions tailored to meet the needs of our clients. In a very short span of its establishment since 2011, Kiran Associates has made a strong repute for impeccable roadways cargo services in Pakistan, which is characterized by Timeliness, Flexibility, Swiftness, Safety, and Reliability. Today we are in a position to provide all sorts of cargo handling related services under one roof. We have attained complete confidence from clients who, after assigning their cargo to us, take a back seat and wait for smooth and timely delivery which is done by us with a complete sense of responsibility. Today, our presence is conspicuous in both the Bonded Transshipment (TP) as well as the Afghan Transit Trade (ATT). This can be primarily credited to our "Well Planned Services," "Mode of Operation," "Resourceful Network," "Talented Workforce," and "Business Ethics." The combination of global reach and local knowledge is a key competitive edge.
